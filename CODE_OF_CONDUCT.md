# Code Of Conduct

## Commitment

This project aims to provide a respectful, inclusive, and constructive collaboration environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in all interactions.
- Focus on technical issues and actionable feedback.
- Assume good intent and seek clarification before escalation.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language.
- Personal attacks, threats, or intimidation.
- Deliberate disclosure of sensitive information.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove or reject contributions that violate this policy and may restrict access for repeat violations.
